📣 1. Marketing Communications

USH Spa may send you marketing communications to keep you informed about:

  • New Services: Announcements about new spa treatments, wellness programs, and beauty services added to our menu.
  • Seasonal Promotions: Special discounts and packages tailored to seasons, holidays, and special occasions.
  • Exclusive Member Offers: Personalized deals based on your booking history, preferences, and loyalty tier.
  • Event Invitations: Exclusive invitations to spa events, wellness workshops, and VIP experiences.
  • Service Updates: Important changes to our services, operating hours, or policies that may affect your experience.

✨ We only send marketing messages with your consent. You can opt out at any time — see Section 7 for details.

🎁 2. Promotional Offers & Campaigns

We run various promotional campaigns throughout the year to reward our customers:

2.1 Seasonal Campaigns

Look out for special offers during:

  • Ramadan & Eid celebrations
  • National Day festivities
  • Summer wellness packages
  • Year-end holiday specials
  • Valentine's Day couples' treatments

2.2 First-Time Customer Offers

New customers receive a welcome discount on their first booking through the USH Spa app. This offer is automatically applied upon account registration and is valid for a limited time.

2.3 Bundle Packages

Save more when you book multiple services together. Our curated bundles combine complementary treatments at discounted rates for a complete wellness experience.

⭐ 3. Loyalty & Rewards Program

Our loyalty program rewards you for every visit and booking:

🥉
Bronze Tier
Earn 1 point per KWD spent. Unlock after your first booking.
🥈
Silver Tier
1.5x points multiplier. Priority booking access and birthday rewards.
🥇
Gold Tier
2x points multiplier. Exclusive services, early access to promotions.
💎
Platinum Tier
3x points multiplier. VIP experiences, complimentary upgrades, and more.

Points can be redeemed for discounts on future bookings, complimentary add-on services, or exclusive merchandise.

🤝 4. Referral Program

Share the USH Spa experience with friends and family and earn rewards:

  • Refer a Friend: Share your unique referral code with friends. When they complete their first booking, both of you receive loyalty points.
  • No Limit: There's no cap on how many friends you can refer — the more you share, the more you earn.
  • Easy Sharing: Share your referral code directly through the app via WhatsApp, SMS, or social media.

🎀 5. Gift Cards

Give the gift of wellness with USH Spa gift cards:

  • Digital Gift Cards: Purchase and send gift cards instantly via our app. Recipients receive an SMS with their unique gift card code.
  • Custom Amounts: Choose any value that suits your budget.
  • Personal Messages: Add a personalized message to make your gift extra special.
  • Easy Redemption: Recipients can redeem gift cards directly in the app when booking any service.

📱 6. Communication Channels

We may reach you through the following channels:

  • SMS: Promotional offers, booking confirmations, and appointment reminders sent to your registered phone number.
  • Email: Newsletters, seasonal campaigns, and account-related communications.
  • Push Notifications: Real-time alerts about flash sales, booking updates, and loyalty milestones (if enabled in the app).
  • In-App Messages: Personalized recommendations and offers displayed within the USH Spa app.
  • Social Media: Follow us on Instagram, Twitter/X, Facebook, and TikTok for the latest content and exclusive social-only promotions.

⚙️ 7. Managing Your Preferences

You are always in control of the marketing communications you receive:

  • In-App Settings: Navigate to Profile → Notification Preferences to toggle specific communication categories on or off.
  • Unsubscribe: Every promotional SMS and email includes an unsubscribe option. Simply follow the instructions to opt out.
  • Contact Support: Email us at Info@ushspakw.com with your preference changes.

📌 Please note: Even if you opt out of marketing communications, you will still receive essential transactional messages such as booking confirmations, payment receipts, and security notifications.
